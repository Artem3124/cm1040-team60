Tickets Information

Purchase and Validity:

Tickets can be purchased online through our official Galactic Gateway or at any of the portal stations located at the entrance of the park. Tickets must be used on the date specified at the time of purchase and are non-transferable. Please ensure your travel dates align with your ticket usage to avoid any time-space discrepancies.

Cancellation and Refund Policy:

We understand that even the best-laid plans of interstellar adventurers can go awry. If you need to cancel your expedition, full refunds are available for tickets canceled at least 72 hours before the designated entry time. Cancellations made less than 72 hours before your journey's commencement will be subject to a 50% cancellation fee. Unfortunately, any cancellations made on the day of arrival cannot be refunded.

Weather and Galactic Conditions:

Our park is open come rain or meteor showers! However, in the rare event of a severe galactic storm or other adverse cosmic events, the park may close for the safety of our visitors and crew. In such cases, visitors will be offered a full refund or the option to reschedule their visit to a clearer day.

Entry Requirements:

All space travellers must present a valid ID galaxy pass and their ticket at the entry gate. Digital and printed tickets are accepted. Ensure that your travel documents are in order as we cannot permit entry to those without proper identification.

Park Conduct:

We strive to offer a safe and enjoyable environment for all our visitors. Please follow all posted guidelines and instructions from park operatives. Anti-gravity antics, while fun, should be kept within the safe zones marked around the park.

Special Offers and Promotions:

Keep an eye on our transmission updates via our official comms channels. We often broadcast special rates and last-minute deals for those looking to escape at warp speed on a budget.

Accessibility:

Our park is fully accessible to beings from all planets and dimensions. Should you require special assistance, please contact our Galactic Support Team before your visit.
