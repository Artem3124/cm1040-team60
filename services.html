Services and Amenities:

Child Tracking Bands

Keep track of your little astronauts with our state-of-the-art tracking bands. These comfortable, wearable devices help you monitor your child’s location within the park at all times via our Galaxy Kids Park app.

How to Get One:

Upon entering Galaxy Kids Park, visit our Guest Services booth to pick up and register your child’s tracking band. Our staff will help you set up the Galaxy Kids Park app on your device, pair the band, and show you how to customize settings for your family's needs.

Peace of Mind for Your Visit:

The Child Tracking Bands at Galaxy Kids Park allow your children the freedom to explore in a controlled and secure environment, making your visit stress-free and enjoyable for the whole family. Enjoy the park, indulge in the attractions,and relax knowing your little ones are just a tap away on your device.

Rest and Recharge Pods:

Explore our family-friendly Rest and Recharge Pods, where you can take a break in a quiet, comfortable setting designed to mimic the peaceful expanse of space. Perfect for naps or just a little downtime.

How to Use the Rest and Recharge Pods:

No need for reservations — our pods are available on a first-come, first-serve basis. Simply enter a vacant pod and settle in. Each pod is designed for short-term use, allowing every family the opportunity to enjoy this unique facility.

Ideal for Every Member of the Family:

Whether it's the little ones needing a nap, teenagers seeking a moment to disconnect with some digital content, or parents looking for a moment of peace, the Rest and Recharge Pods are designed to meet the needs of all ages and preferences. At Galaxy Kids Park, we understand that a great day out can be tiring. Our Rest and Recharge Pods provide the perfect space to take a break, ensuring that your family's adventure can continue with everyone feeling refreshed and rejuvenated.

Emergency Medical Services:

How to Access Our Emergency Medical Services

If you require medical assistance, you can visit any of our medical stations directly, or contact any park staff member. For urgent situations, our park is equipped with emergency call points and a dedicated medical response team ready to come to your location within the park.

Rest Assured During Your Visit:

While we hope your visit is free from mishaps, our Emergency Medical Services are here to handle any situation with care and expertise. At Galaxy Kids Park, we are committed to ensuring that your adventure is not only exciting but also safe, letting you focus on making memorable moments with your family.
